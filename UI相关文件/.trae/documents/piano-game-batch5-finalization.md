## Assumptions & Decisions

1. **No `--replace-head` needed**: Head infrastructure is already properly formed. Running `--replace-head` would destroy the Chart.js CDN include in `results.html`'s `<head>`.
2. **Self-link interactions registered with `hideEdge`**: `nav-home` on home page, `nav-library` on song-library page, `nav-settings` on settings page, and `back-home` on home page are self-links. They are registered with `hideEdge: true` to ensure no orphaned `data-dom-id` attributes. This is harmless — clicking in preview stays on the same page.
3. **All 39 data-dom-ids registered**: The original wiringPlan (5) + hiddenInteractionPlan (17) = 22 entries was incomplete. The Sub-Agents added 17 additional data-dom-ids (nav-home, nav-library on most pages, cta-to-follow-play, shortcut-free-play, link-ai-transcription). All are registered to ensure every clickable cross-page control has a corresponding interaction.
4. **Visible wiring = 5 edges only**: The core business flow chain. All auxiliary navigation (back, nav, shortcuts, secondary CTAs) uses `hideEdge: true`.
